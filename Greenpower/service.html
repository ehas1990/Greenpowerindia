Portable Plant

Green Power India offers specially designed portable biogas plants that efficiently process kitchen waste from micro families without adequate space. Constructed with FRP material in accordance with ANERT's specifications and design, these 1m cube plants effectively treat an average household's kitchen waste, providing up to two hours of gas supply. Additionally, the organic manure derived from the process serves as a valuable resource for agriculture. Customers can enjoy the convenience of a five-year warranty on these plants.

Fixed dome biogas plant

Green Power India offers fixed biogas plants that have the capacity to fully meet the cooking needs of an average family. These plants require a space of 10 square meters for installation and come with a 5-year free structural warranty. Our expertise extends to the design, manufacturing, and maintenance of fixed dome plants, available in a range of sizes from 1-meter cube to 60-meter cube. We cater to diverse sectors such as Slaughter Waste, Poultry Waste, Hospitals, Schools, Hotels, Farms, and more, ensuring efficient and eco-friendly biogas solutions for various needs.

Rain Water Harvesting

We are dedicated to conserving precious water resources through our efficient rainwater harvesting solutions. Our skilled team specializes in designing, implementing, and maintaining rainwater harvesting systems for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. With extensive expertise and experience, we guarantee top-notch installations and effective utilization of rainwater. Our customized solutions are tailored to meet your specific requirements, enabling maximum water collection while seamlessly integrating with existing structures and minimizing disruptions. Furthermore, we provide long-term support through regular maintenance and monitoring services, ensuring optimal performance and durability of your rainwater harvesting system. Join us in our mission to conserve water and promote sustainable living. Contact Green Power India today and let's make every drop count towards a greener future.

STP and ETP

Green Power India specializes in comprehensive wastewater treatment solutions through our Sewage Treatment Plants (STP) and Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP). We offer customized STP systems for the efficient treatment of domestic and industrial sewage, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. Our ETP solutions effectively purify industrial effluents, incorporating advanced processes and technologies. Benefits include compliance, contaminant removal, resource recovery, cost efficiency, and expert support. Contact us to discuss your wastewater treatment needs and contribute to water conservation and environmental stewardship.
